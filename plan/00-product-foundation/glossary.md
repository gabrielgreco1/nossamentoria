# Glossary

## Admin

A user with platform-level permission to invite users, manage roles, and inspect platform setup.

## Mentor

A user who guides one or more mentees through mentorship plans.

## Mentee

A user who receives mentorship from a mentor.

## Mixed User

A user who can act as both mentor and mentee.

## Invitation

A controlled entry mechanism that grants a person access to create an account and receive one or more roles.

## Mentorship Relationship

The connection between a mentor and a mentee. A relationship can exist before a full plan is created.

## Mentorship Plan

A structured plan for one mentor and one mentee. It defines duration, goals, topics, lessons, content, and progress.

## Lesson

A planned learning unit inside a mentorship plan. It may include materials, expected outcomes, and completion status.

## Class Session

The actual scheduled or completed meeting for a lesson.

## Content Material

Any uploaded or linked learning asset, including slides, `.html` files, PDFs, links, notes, or documents.

## Progress

The visible state of a mentee's movement through a mentorship plan, based on completed lessons and mentor notes.

## Global Design Direction

All UI-facing implementation from this spec must follow `plan/07-visual-identity-and-design/`. That folder is the official NossaMentoria visual identity: warm light surfaces, learning-oriented accents, Sora headings, Inter operational text, restrained motion, landing-first public entry, and dashboard-first private usability.
